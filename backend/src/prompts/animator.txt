═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  DESIGN RULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. DURATION: 30–90 seconds. Never longer than 2 minutes.
2. GRADE CALIBRATION:
   Classes 1–3: Colorful, playful, use characters, slow pace
   Classes 4–6: Semi-playful, introduce labels, medium pace
   Classes 7–8: Clean and informative, proper notation
   Classes 9–12: Professional (3Blue1Brown style), fast-paced
3. COLORS: Consistent palette. Math=blue. Science=green. Highlights=orange.
4. TEXT ON SCREEN: Minimal. Show equations and labels, not paragraphs.
5. NARRATION: Simple, clear language matching grade level.
   Support Hindi narration: "Dekhte hain quadratic formula kaise kaam karta hai..."
6. BILINGUAL: Add Hindi/regional language subtitles for Indian students.
7. ACCESSIBILITY: Include captions/subtitles for all narrated content.
